Strategic Recommendations

7 plays to
grow faster

Based on traffic gaps, audience demographics, financial data, and competitive benchmarks — here are the highest-leverage digital moves for Swiggy.

01

Activate the Email Channel

Email is the most underutilised source. Targeting 14.3M transacting users with personalised re-order nudges could drive 5–8% revenue uplift with minimal spend.

Retention
02

Diversify Traffic Sources

56.9% direct traffic means brand strength, but fragility. Invest in Google Display, YouTube Pre-roll, and affiliate channels to de-risk acquisition concentration.

Acquisition
03

Instagram-First Social Strategy

Social mix is LinkedIn-heavy. Instagram's Gen-Z + Millennial audience better matches Swiggy's 18–34 growth cohort — a channel that drives intent, not just awareness.

Social
04

Instamart Checkout Optimisation

Instamart delivers $1B GOV vs Food's $3B. A/B test bundle offers and upsell nudges on the web checkout funnel to close the gap on average order value.

Conversion
05

Dineout SEO Push

The dining vertical contributes only $0.3B GOV. Restaurant-search keywords cost as little as $0.15 CPC — low-hanging organic traffic with strong commercial intent.

SEO
06

Fix Instamart Exit Funnel

28% of post-visit traffic goes to google.com, signalling unmet search intent on grocery queries. Improve landing page relevance and internal search quality.

UX
07

Profitability-Linked GA4 Events

Instrument GA4 custom events for reorder, subscription signup, and cross-vertical purchase to directly tie web analytics metrics to the path to EBITDA breakeven.
